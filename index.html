Israeli Reinforcement Learning workshop



June 30th 2022, NVIDIA Tel Aviv offices, Yitzhak Sadeh 4 (Building A)


We are thrilled to announce the second Israeli Reinforcement Learning workshop, an academic meetup of researchers and Reinforcement Learning in Israeli academia. The event will take place physically on Thursday 30/6/2022, and will include talks by invited speakers and short presentations of recent work in the field by Israeli researchers.
